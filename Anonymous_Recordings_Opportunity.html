Confidential Shamrock Investment Opportunity

Executive Summary

We recommend Shamrock pursue the Anonymous Recordings opportunity based on its stable earnings profile, diversified streaming growth, alignment with Shamrock's media strategy, and positioning across high-demand genres (R&B, hip-hop, pop).

The $44 million purchase price implies a 11.1x entry multiple (based on 2021–2022 average earnings), projecting a 13.5% levered IRR and 2.4x MOIC over a 9-year hold.

Key Highlights

  • Streaming Growth Offset: Despite a 32% decline in mechanical royalties in 2022 and 24% in 2023, streaming earnings grew 90% and 64% in those years, stabilizing total earnings at ~$3.95M annually.
  • Portfolio Diversification: No single album exceeds 11.7% of earnings; the top 10 represent 60%, indicating moderate concentration.
  • Debt Service Coverage: Mechanical royalties (~$9.2M from 2021–2023) support near-term debt service, while streaming growth supports long-term returns.

We recommend Shamrock prepare a diligence request list and submit a letter of interest to the seller to initiate confirmatory diligence.

Key Risk Exposure Analysis

Primary Risk Factors

Streaming Revenue Concentration

Current streaming revenue is heavily concentrated among a few major platforms. If these platforms change their royalty models or payment structures, it could significantly impact projected returns.

Mitigation: Diversification of exploitation channels and active exploitation management

Mechanical Royalty Acceleration

While mechanical royalties are declining at an expected rate, an accelerated decline beyond forecast would pressure near-term cash flows.

Mitigation: Streaming growth buffer and conservative debt structuring

Genre Obsolescence

While hip-hop/R&B and pop are currently strong performers, tastes can change. If these genres lose popularity, it could affect streaming numbers.

Mitigation: Catalog includes diverse artists/styles within genres; multiple albums with demonstrated evergreen appeal
